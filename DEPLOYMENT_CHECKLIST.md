## Emergency Rollback

If issues occur after launch:
1. Go to Online Store > Themes
2. Find previous theme under "Theme library"
3. Click "Actions" > "Publish"
4. Previous theme will be live immediately
5. Review issues with new theme
6. Test fixes on unpublished theme
7. Re-publish when ready
